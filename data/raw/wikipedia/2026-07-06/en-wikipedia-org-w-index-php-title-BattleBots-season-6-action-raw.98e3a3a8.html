{{Use American English|date=March 2026}} {{Use mdy dates|date=September 2016}} {{Infobox television season | image = Battlebots_(ABC)_(season_1)_poster.jpg | image_size = 250px | bgcolour = #A62821 | caption = | num_episodes = 6 | network = [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] | first_aired = {{Start date|2015|06|21}} | last_aired = {{End date|2015|07|26}} | prev_season = [[BattleBots season 5|Season 5]] | next_season = [[BattleBots season 7|Season 7]] | episode_list = BattleBots | season_number = 6 | image_alt = | starring = | released = }} The sixth season of the American competitive television series ''[[BattleBots]]'' premiered on [[American Broadcasting Company|ABC]] on June 21, 2015. This season is also referred to as Season 1, as it is the first season after it was rebooted. In February 2015, ABC announced that it was reviving the series after a thirteen-year hiatus, which would feature a 16-team single-elimination tournament.{{cite web|title=ABC Revives 'BattleBots' As Summer Reality Series|url=https://deadline.com/2015/02/battlebots-revival-reality-series-abc-summer-1201367663/|website=Deadline|date=February 6, 2015 |accessdate=February 6, 2015}} Former UFC fighter [[Kenny Florian]] and MLB/NFL Sportscaster [[Chris Rose]] hosted, Faruq Tauheed acted as the arena announcer, and [[Alison Haislip]] was the sideline and behind the scenes reporter. [[Molly McGrath]] was the presenter. Robots in season 6 competed in one 250-pound weight class, as opposed to the multiple weight classes in seasons 1–5. ==Contestants== This season sees teams from only two countries competing with their robots. Five from the United Kingdom and the rest from the United States.
